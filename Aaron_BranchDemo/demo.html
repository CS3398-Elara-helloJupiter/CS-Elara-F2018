TAB Application

Created by: Mary Gutierrez, Aaron Hunt, Jessica Cruz, Samantha Hollensbe, and Louis Scinta

User Guide: When finding the desired button click enter, to go into the category section press the number 4 key. To navigate inside the category section; to go to the next button use "tab" key, to go back a button use shift+ tab key. to delete a category or subcategory double click on the desired button to delete. To go back into a previous level: press shift + 3 (subject level), or 4(category level), 5(table) After finding the desired sub column press enter and then press the number 5 key, to navigate to the table. Level 5 contains a table Column from left to right it shows, task name, description and due date. To navigate through the columns, press the tab key.
